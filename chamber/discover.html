Tourist Attractions

Tinapa Park: Tinapa Resort is a captivating business and leisure destination located north of Calabar, Cross River State, Nigeria. This lively area features a casino, digital cinema, children's arcade, restaurants, a mini amphitheater, a nightclub, and pubs.

Obodu Cattle Ranch: The Obudu Cattle Ranch enjoys a semi-temperate mountain climate due to its elevation of 1,600 meters (5,200 feet). This makes it a serene escape with pleasant weather compared to other regions in Nigeria. The ranch has become a popular holiday and tourist resort center, attracting both Nigerian and international visitors. Its deep tropical forests, highlands, and tropical temperate climate contribute to its allure. Visitors can explore attractions like the cable car, waterfalls, and breathtaking mountain views.

Water Park: Perfect for family fun, this water park provides a refreshing escape with various water-based activities.

Tidal Lake: An artificial tidal lake fed by the Calabar River adds to the scenic beauty of Tinapa.

Tinapa Zoo: Embark on a unique safari tour to learn about animal habitats and behaviors.
